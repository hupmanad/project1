Andrew's Homepage

Welcome to Andrew's new website! Today, I want to showcase some of the games I played in my life that I really like.
Now, someone may wonder, why would you do this? Who cares? Well, recently I realized that while I enjoy a lot of
games, I often don't know how to really put my appreciation for them into words. If I wanted to describe why I
enjoy something, saying it's fun is a great start! But I also want to be able to go into greater detail on why
extactly I think they're fun. Plus, it's nice to imagine other people seeing this and wanting to try these ones
for themselves if they haven't already. Personally, I find them to be some of the best I have played, and since
a lot of them are a bit older it would be great to know that other people are starting to play them again.
